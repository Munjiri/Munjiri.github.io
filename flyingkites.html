FLYING KITES

Flying Kites believe in providing students with access to high­ quality education and holistic support is vital to alleviate suffering, increase gender equality and promote economic sustainability. By meeting the needs of the whole child, their integrative programs give students the tools they need to build their best lives and positively impact their families, communities and country.
