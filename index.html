M U N J I R I  V I D E O S

We are two freelance videographers working between the UK and South Africa
(and some places inbetween) telling your stories through authentic short films.

With an interest in creativity, conservation and community, our videos tell your unique story, creating a real connection with people and helping your organisation grow. We understand that marketing is simply storytelling and the key to growing your organisation will be to create stories: true stories, helpful stories, beautiful stories and video is an amazing way to do this.

Katy studied Communication Design, specialising in film at Glasgow School of Art in Scotland and Eugene studied Field Guiding (safari guiding) in Limpopo, South Africa. We met in the South Luangwa National Park in Zambia, when we were both working there and started Munjiri Videos. Munjiri means warthog in the local language and if you have ever watched a warthog run you will know how magnificent it is!
