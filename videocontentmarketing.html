Is It Time To Add Video To Your Content Marketing?

Many people think video marketing is nice to have, but not necessarily a need to have, your competitors will all have a social media presence, email marketing, and a blog, but they may not have video marketing, so, why bother? As the market becomes more and more saturated, it's important to differentiate from the pack. 

Video Builds Trust

In our videos we encourage the people we work with to be themselves and we often film at their place of work or in their home. Going behind the scenes builds trust. The point of marketing is to build trust and create long-term relationships. Pushy selling can give you and other people the ick, let people come to you by providing them interesting and useful information.

Video Boosts Conversions and Sales

If someone lands on your page and can’t figure out what it is that you do, that’s when bounces happen. Adding a video on your landing page can increase conversions by 80%. 

If you think about it, the effectiveness of video is not even that surprising. After all, vision is our most dominant sense. Most information transmitted to our brain is visual. So if already pictures can boost engagement massively, imagine what moving pictures can do to your business.

Our brain loves video because it is programmed to retain visuals better than a page loaded with words. According to studies, the average viewer remembers 95% of a message when it is watched, whereas only 10% when read.

Video is a great tool for learning, but it’s also very easy to take in. Today’s life is super busy and people don't always have time to read a long description or dig deep into what you do. People want to see your product or service in action.

Google Loves Videos

Videos increase the time visitors spend on your site. Longer exposure builds trust and signals search engines that your site has good content. You’re 53 times more likely to show up first on Google if you have a video embedded on your website.

Always remember to optimize your videos for SEO. Write interesting titles and descriptions. Add a link back to your website, products, and services and give potential customers a way to take the next step.
