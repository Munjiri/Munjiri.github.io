SOUL DESIGN

Down a cobbled street in Bo-Kaap, Cape Town is Abi's home and studio, where the beautiful Soul Design Jewellery is based. Soul wanted a videographer to create a short film for their website telling their story. A story about travelling, collaborating and collecting, but also one about home. Soul's unique jewellery is handmade using local materials and each piece of their jewellery, emphasizes the unique skills of their artisans.

AFRICAN BAT CONSERVATION

Having finished a few videography projects in Cape Town we headed to Malawi to capture a video about the work of African Bat Conservation. Mostly we hear stories about human wildlife conflict, but just outside Lilongwe these bats are helping to create an excellent banana harvest. Filming in a banana field in the dark was interesting and seeing these lil bats up close was amazing!

AJIRI TEA

Ajiri needed a video that would clearly convey their brand vision and message as their brand had a number of different aspects to it. Ajiri aims to create employment and support orphan education through sales of their award winning tea and coffee. The labels and twines are made from dried banana bark, so with this employment women in the community are able to earn an income. The income has really helped them improve their standards of living for example, the women have electricity in their homes and they are now able to take their kids to school.

KUSINI SAFARIS

Kusini Safaris (previously Paul Tickner Safaris) wanted to create a video that captured what happens on their safaris,  the fascinating nature, the fun moments with your family and their commitment to conservation. Kusini Safaris believes that responsible tourism can help to solve the problems of wildlife conservation and economic and social development. Their safaris have a strong commitment to this; promoting wild places, using good ethical practice and working with local communities.

MUSHROOM FARM

The Mushroom Farm in northern Malawi is a permaculture eco-lodge, campsite and social enterprise. We met with Bishop and Joshua to chat more about the lodge, ate delicious vegetarian food, had a cliff side shower with epic views of the Livingstonia escarpment and took a stroll to Manchewe Falls. Mushroom Farm also supports the local nursery, runs adult literacy classes and have just started a very cool jewelry making project.

CORAL AND HIVE

Coral and Hive produces beautiful handmade rugs in Cape Town, South Africa. Their hand woven natural rugs are made from a range of fibres including mohair, cotton, karakul, wool and hemp. They employ some of the most talented weavers in South Africa, whose skills have been passed down for generations. Nazeema their head weaver, has been weaving for over 30 years.

UWEZA

Uweza recognises that Kibera residents are creative, resilient and resourceful and that sustainable change requires a future generation that is more educated, motivated and empowered. They provide 300 Kenyan children and youth with unique opportunities to pursue a path to a better future through the discovery and development of their talents and abilities.

NYALETI LODGE

Nyaleti Lodge and Villa are situated in the beautiful Hoedspruit Wildlife Reserve. Run by two wildlife conservationists, Nyaleti Lodge is the ideal place to stay if you are looking to explore the beautiful Kruger National Park, Blyde River Canyon and eat a lot of delicious waffles and pancakes for breakfast. Being situated in a wildlife estate means that you can walk freely amongst the warthogs and giraffes without the fear of being eaten by a lion (there are no dangerous predators in the reserve) as well as spot the amazing birdlife from the bird hides that are dotted around the reserves waterholes.
